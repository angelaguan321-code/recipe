Sushi
Instructions

Step 1

Gather your ingredients and lay down a half sheet of nori

Step 2

Spread rice on the nori and sprinkle some sesame seeds

Step 3

Add your fillings like crab, avocado, and cucumber

Step 4

Lastly, roll it up and slice the sushi

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Crab
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame Seeds
